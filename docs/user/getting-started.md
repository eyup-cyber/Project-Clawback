## Creating Your Account

### Sign Up Options

You can create an account using:

1. **Email and Password**
   - Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
   - Enter your email address
   - Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Verify your email address

2. **Social Login**
   - Click "Sign Up with Google" or other available providers
   - Authorize access to your basic profile information
   - Your account will be created automatically

3. **Magic Link**
   - Enter your email address
   - Click "Send Magic Link"
   - Check your email and click the link to sign in

### Email Verification

After signing up with email, you'll receive a verification email. Click the link to verify your account. This helps us:

- Ensure you own the email address
- Enable account recovery features
- Send important notifications

## Setting Up Your Profile

After creating your account, customize your profile:

### Profile Information

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Profile**
2. Add the following:
   - **Display Name**: How others will see you
   - **Username**: Your unique @handle (can't be changed later)
   - **Bio**: Tell others about yourself
   - **Avatar**: Upload a profile picture

### Preferences

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Preferences**
2. Customize:
   - **Theme**: Light, dark, or system
   - **Language**: Choose your preferred language
   - **Notifications**: Configure email and push notifications
   - **Content**: Set your content preferences

## Becoming a Contributor

Ready to share your own content? Here's how to become a contributor:

### Apply for Contributor Status

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Apply to Create**
2. Fill out the application:
   - Why you want to contribute
   - What type of content you'll create
   - Links to previous work (if any)
3. Submit your application

### Application Review

- Applications are typically reviewed within 48 hours
- You'll receive an email with the decision
- If approved, you'll gain access to the creator dashboard

### Once Approved

As a contributor, you can:

- Create and publish content
- Access analytics for your posts
- Build your audience
- Earn recognition

For detailed guidance, see the [Contributor Guide](/docs/user/contributor-guide.md).

## Security Features

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add extra security to your account:

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Security**
2. Click "Enable 2FA"
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app
4. Enter the verification code
5. Save your backup codes securely

### Session Management

View and manage your active sessions:

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Security**
2. See all active sessions with device info
3. Revoke any suspicious sessions

### Privacy Settings

Control your privacy:

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Privacy**
2. Configure:
   - Profile visibility (public/private)
   - Data collection preferences
   - Marketing communications
